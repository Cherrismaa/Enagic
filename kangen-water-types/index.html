Types of Kangen Water

Kangen Water comes in five unique types, ranging from pH 2.5 to 11.5 each crafted for a specific purpose. The unique benefits of each type of Kangen Water, designed to enhance your health, beauty, and daily life.

Strong Kangen Water

pH 11.0

Powerful cleaning water. Not for drinking. Preserves hygiene in your daily life due to its strong cleaning effect.

Strong Kangen Water

Not for drinking. Powerful cleaning and dissolving properties make it ideal for food preparation and sanitation.


Food Prep: Removes rawness from onions, wasabi, etc.

Cleaning: Dissolves oil & grime from surfaces.

Stains: Removes soy sauce, coffee, and toilet stains.

Dishes: Saves detergent and water.

Kangen Water®

pH 8.5 - 9.5

Perfect for daily hydration. Hydrogen-rich water works to restore your body to a more alkaline state.

Kangen Water®

For drinking and cooking. Restores your body’s alkaline balance, improves taste, and hydrates deeply.


Drinking: Clean taste, no odor. Sweet, fresh hydration all day.

Cooking & Food Prep: Enhances flavor and freshness of vegetables, less seasoning needed.

Coffee & Tea: Richer color, aroma, and taste using less quantity.

Plants: Boosts germination and growth of plants.

Clean Water

pH 7.0

Pure, clean water ideal for baby formula and taking medications. Free of chlorine, rust and cloudiness.

Clean Water

Neutral and chlorine-free. Ideal for baby food and medications where purity is essential.


Baby Food: Gentle water for preparing formula.

Medication: Easily absorbed for tablets and pills..

Beauty Water

pH 4.0 - 6.0

Excellent for cosmetic uses, skin care and hair washing. Slightly acidic water is recognized for its astringent effects.

Beauty Water

Not for drinking. Excellent for skincare, hair care, pets, and natural cleaning.


Face Wash: Tones skin and closes pores after cleansing.

Hair Care: Post-shampoo rinse for shine and detangling.

Pets: Spray for glossy, soft fur.

Polishing: Use on mirrors, glasses, and windows.

Frozen Food: Spray before freezing to preserve flavor.

Strong Acidic Water

pH 2.5

Disinfecting properties. Used for sanitizing utensils, hands, and surfaces more to prevent cross-contamination

Strong Acidic Water

Not for drinking. A powerful disinfectant used for hygiene, cleaning, and commercial sanitation.


Hygiene: Clean hands, toothbrushes, or use in spray bottles.

Commercial: Used in salons, restaurants, hospitals, daycares.

Germ Control: Disinfect knives, boards, cloths, and surfaces.
