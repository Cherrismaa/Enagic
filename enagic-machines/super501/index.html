Overview

The Super 501 is meticulously engineered to meet the demands of active, wellness-focused families and small businesses that require a high-capacity, versatile water solution. This top-of-the-line unit produces all five types of Enagic water, and features dual electrode plates and a built-in electrolysis enhancer tank.

ORP: -800 mV

One of the most powerful negative ORP ratings among Enagic machines, delivering exceptional antioxidant potential.

pH Range

Alkaline 8.5 – 11.5
Acidic 2.5 – 6.0

Health Benefits

  • High-output Kangen Water® for large families or businesses
  • Produces antioxidant-rich water for optimal hydration
  • Delivers versatile water types for cooking, cleaning, beauty, and sanitization
  • Supports high-usage environments with reliable flow rate

One Ionizer, Five Functional Waters

Produces Strong Kangen Water®, Kangen Water®, Clean Water, Beauty Water, and Strong Acidic Water—catering to all health and household needs.

User-Friendly Operation

Select the water you need with a single button. The machine confirms selections via voice, and gives filter replacement alerts automatically.

Advanced Electrode Technology

Features a dual system with 7 platinum-coated plates for producing Strong Acidic and Strong Kangen Water®, and 5 plates for the rest. Reliable for industrial-level output.

High Capacity & Industrial Strength

Generates up to 2 gallons per minute of Kangen Water®. The built-in electrolysis enhancer tank supports high-demand usage efficiently.

Machine Technology

The Super 501 is the most powerful unit in the Enagic lineup. With 12 total plates (7+5), dual electrolysis systems, and a powerful enhancer tank, it delivers consistent, high-volume output while maintaining strong ORP and pH levels.


Dual Electrolysis Plates: 7 plates + 5 plates for optimized function
