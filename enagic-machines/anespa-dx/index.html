Overview

The ANESPA DX is an exclusive home spa system that transforms your regular shower into a refreshing, mineral-rich hot spring experience. It produces a continuous stream of healthy ionized mineral water with a powerful ceramic cartridge that removes nearly 100% of chlorine and other harmful substances, while infusing water with natural minerals beneficial for your skin and hair.

Benefits

  • Removes chlorine, bacteria, and harmful substances using active charcoal and ceramic filtration
  • Infuses water with moisturizing hot spring minerals
  • Gentle on skin and hair, perfect for sensitive skin
  • Produces mineral ion water for showers and baths
  • Includes massage shower head for a luxurious spa experience

Your Home Spa System

Enjoy the soothing feeling of being in a Japanese hot spring with ANESPA DX. It includes a relaxing massage shower head and abundant flow of mineralized water, turning your bathroom into a spa-like oasis.

Removes Harmful Substances

The advanced ceramic and charcoal filter removes chlorine and bacteria effectively, helping protect delicate skin and offering an invigorating bathing experience. Built with a larger ceramic cartridge and an upgraded base for easier maintenance.

Mineral Ion Water

Infused with a blend of natural minerals from the Futamata Radium Hot Spring in Hokkaido, including:

  • Tufa: Creates a calming, hot spring effect
  • MIC Stone: Generates mildly alkaline water for healthy skin
  • Power Stone: Releases strong negative ions
