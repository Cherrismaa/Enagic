The Leveluk JR IV is an economical starter model featuring 4 solid platinum-coated titanium plates, making it suitable for small families or individuals. Despite its compact size, it produces 5 types of Enagic water including Kangen Water®.

The Junior Model

The JrIV uses four solid platinum-coated titanium plates, which cut energy usage while still generating strong acid and strong Kangen® waters, this machine is considered one of Enagic's starter models.

Generates: Strong Kangen Water, Kangen Water®, Neutral Water, Beauty Water, and Strong Acidic Water.

JrIV Control Panel

With just a touch of a button, you can have access to any of the 4 waters you choose. Your machine will confirm the water you have selected and in moments the machine will generate healthy, ionized water.

Your JrIV will even notify you when it's time to change the water filter via a message on the LCD Panel and a buzzer sound.

4 Titanium Plates

The JrIV has four solid electrode plates which reduces energy consumption. Due to the solid platinum-coated titanium plates, this unit can still produce the strong acid and strong Kangen waters.

This machine is ideal for singles or couples due to its compact build and lower power consumption.

Electrolysis Enhancer Tank

The built-in tank for the electrolysis enhancer saves you the trouble of manual setup. It's essential for obtaining strong acid and strong Kangen Water.

This built-in tank allows continuous and stable production of specialty waters with excellent effects.

Machine Technology

Equipped with 4 large platinum-coated titanium plates, the JR IV efficiently electrolyzes tap water to produce high-quality ionized water. Though it has fewer plates than other models, it's powerful enough for daily use and compact in size.


Electrolysis Plates: 4 solid platinum-coated titanium
