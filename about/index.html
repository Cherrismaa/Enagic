What Makes Enagic Different ?

Enagic’s commitment to excellence is rooted in Japanese precision, technology, and innovation. From proprietary filtration systems to global support, we go beyond the ordinary to offer extraordinary benefits for your health and environment.

History

In 1974 in Okinawa, Japan, Sony’s trading operation, the precursor of Enagic, began operations. Ever since, Enagic's commitment to high‑quality products, innovation and the True Health philosophy has made it the leader in water ionizer generating systems, now distributed globally.

New offices continue to open worldwide as the company expands and reaches more and more people, providing healthy, alkaline water and a way to improve their finances as well.

Locations

Enagic continues to grow and expand its operations. Just in the past three years new offices have been established in India, Russia and Brazil.

Plans are underway to develop new Enagic® centers in about 20 other countries in the next few years. With presence in 35 locations across 25 countries and growing, you can be sure to get all the support you need from this global, well‑established company.
