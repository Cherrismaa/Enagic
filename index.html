What is Kangen Water?

Ionized Kangen Water is a clean, alkaline, antioxidant-rich water produced by Enagic’s ionization machines.

Kangen Water ranges in pH from 8.5 to 11.5 and contains reduced molecular hydrogen with a strong negative oxidation-reduction potential (ORP).
